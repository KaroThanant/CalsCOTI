Welcome in this little corner of the Internet! Make yourself cosy, I'll be right here !

Hi ! I'm Calgeka, French part-time artist, part-time developper and full-time ADHD. I'm always looking for new challenges and experiments ! I have a hobby list longer than my arm, which is apparently still not enough.

Designer

I made this site not only as a showcase to my art, but also as a challenge to myself. It took a lot of work to be proud of myself, and proud of this website. But as it is with life, the journey continues. As I learn more, I will keep to improve this little corner of the Internet.

I do not do art for the beauty of itself. I'd rather it have an impact on people's lives. And what better impact that using art to design websites that are both beautiful and easy to use ?

I place the user experience at the heart of my work. I strive to make all my designs daltonian-friendly, and compatible with neurodivergent and disabled people. Should you notice any possible amelioration, shoot me a message, or for the tech-savy, you can open a pull-request on GitHub ! I will do all I can to help you !
